Easy Origami

We have plenty of origami models with the Easy difficulty rating.

Some “easy” origami models are not too easy, which means if you're a complete beginner, you may want to have a go at some origami base folds for beginners first.

Our origami models that are categorised as “easy” also include models that are “easy to intermediate”. Please view each individual tutorial to see the detailed difficulty level.

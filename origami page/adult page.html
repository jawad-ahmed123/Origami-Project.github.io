Hard Origami

Not too hard, but not easy either, you’ll find some fancy boxes, stars and pretty flowers among other things that will take some mental effort here.

A small note, the tutorials posted here are never “too hard”, if you’re willing and patient, most people can fold along. We have never had any origami model with any epic-too-hard-to-make level.

These origami models also include the difficulty rating of “Intermediate to Advanced” origami models. Please view each individual tutorial to see the detailed difficulty level.

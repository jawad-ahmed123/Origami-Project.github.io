Origami Frame Box Tutorial – Wide Rimmed Box

These are the instructions on how to make an origami frame box. You can stand these boxes up and use them as picture frames for your photos or art, or use them as pretty display boxes. They are great to use as shadow boxes too. Made from 1 sheet of square paper, no glue required.

You can also make a little masu box to go in the middle. See below for sizes.

What You Need

  • - 1 sheet of square paper. Paper size example: 21 x 21 cm
  • - Final model size: 10 x 10 x 2.5 cm. Inner Box: 5 x 5 x 2.5 cm
  • - Paper size: 14.5 x 14.5 cm (if used 21 x 21 cm for frame box)
  • - For other sizes, times your inner box size by 2.9 (e.g: inner box size: 6 x 6 cm, so, 6 x 2.9 = 17.4.. so you’d need 17.4 x 17.4 cm).
  • - Other Tools: Scissors, ruler, etc.

Foods Origami

Feeling hungry? Check out some of these cute origami food tutorials.

These include origami sushi, origami fruit, origami vegetables, origami candy, origami ice-cream, origami cake and more.

Food themed origami is great for parties and special events. Use them to decorate greeting cards or invitations. Use the food themed boxes to give gifts and decorate the table.

Make an origami pumpkin box. This Halloween gift box is perfect to put your candy corns in. Made from just 2 sheets of square paper.

Origami Accordion folding

On this page you’ll find all posts that are categorised as Accordion Origami, which means the origami model uses accordion folding techniques.

Origami Accordion Folding is any origami model that resembles an accordion – a box shaped musical instrument that expands and contracts with air coming in and out to make the sound.

Accordion origami is often modular, being used for origami vases, books, hanging decorations, lamp shades, folders and more.

Origami boxes

The number one most popular kind of origami – origami boxes! Well we think so anyway, they are popular with everyone here.

Origami boxes are great for so many different occasions. You can use them for everyday purposes or as decorations. If you use the right paper they can be very sturdy and useful.

Boxes make great gifts and also gift boxes to put special presents inside, such as jewellery, money and more.

Origami Envelopes

On this page you can find all of our origami tutorials in the Origami Envelopes category.

Origami envelopes are not only pretty but useful too. Although it’s not recommended to put a stamp on one and send it through the postal service – you can use them to personalise all kinds of gifts.

Another use is to store documents and other small items as well as hand deliver part invitations, wedding invitations, or thank you cards.

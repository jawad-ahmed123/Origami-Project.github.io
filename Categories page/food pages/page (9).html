Round Origami Candy Box Instructions

With this tutorial you can learn how to make a round origami candy box, this time the ‘fans’ of the candy are pointy, or you can leave them straight. You can make the fans of the candy a different colour to the box! Or you could use pin stripe paper for a classic candy look.

This origami candy box is modular, it stays together very well, you won’t need glue, you can if you want, maybe if you want them to be handled a lot.

What You Need

  • - 2 sheets of 15 x 15 cm.
  • - 1 sheet of paper, 21 x 21 cm.
  • - Other Tools: Scissors, ruler, etc.
